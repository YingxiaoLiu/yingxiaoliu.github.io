Our research group is looking for fully funded Ph.D. students in geomechanics and computational mechanics.

Successful applicants will work on research topics related to computational modeling, geomaterials, material behavior, and numerical simulation methods. Students with strong backgrounds in mechanics, numerical methods, and programming are especially encouraged to apply.

Basic Requirements
======

Applicants should have:
* A bachelor’s degree in mechanics, mechanical engineering, civil engineering, materials science, or a related field;
* Programming experience with Python or C++;
* Basic knowledge and experience in finite element methods, material point methods, or other numerical simulation methods;
* Fulfillment of the general Ph.D. admission requirements listed by Rowan University: https://global.rowan.edu/programs/phd-in-engineering.html

Preferred Qualifications
=====
Preference will be given to applicants with:
* A master’s degree in a related field;
* Research experience in numerical simulation methods;
* Programming experience with TensorFlow, PyTorch, or other machine learning frameworks.

How to Apply
=====
Interested applicants should send their application materials to liuyin@rowan.edu with the email subject:
<i>Ph.D. Application + Applicant Name</i>
